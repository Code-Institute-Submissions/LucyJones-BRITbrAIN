How to play

You might think you're good at quizzes, but how well do you cope under pressure?

The BRITbrAIN quiz will test your British knowledge but with only 30 seconds to answer as many questions as you can. Read the question and select the correct region on the map to score points. Each correct answer will score you 10 points. However be warned - if you get a question wrong, it will cost you 5 points!
